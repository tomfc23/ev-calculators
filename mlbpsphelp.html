MLB PSP help

  1. Go to safari then sportsbook.fanduel.com
  2. Go to desired game and expand the box for "To record an RBI"
  3. Screenshot then hit the full page button near the top.
  4. Safe that to your photos then crop the full page screenshot to just be the rbi odds.
  5. Use the built in text-recognition to copy and paste similar to DOTD.
  6. If on android, look up how to take a "scrolling screenshot" or "full-page screenshot" then have chatgpt or some other ai convert the rbi odds section to text in the following format: "Enter players and odds (one per line, alternating name and odds)"
